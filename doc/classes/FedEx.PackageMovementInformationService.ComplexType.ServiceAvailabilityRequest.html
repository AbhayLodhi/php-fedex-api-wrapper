    • \ServiceAvailabilityRequest

    The descriptive data which is used to determine which FedEx Express services are available between an origin and destination.

    To check the availability of one particular FedEx Express service and packaging type, include the Service and Packaging elements in the request message. Only information regarding that single service and packaging type will be returned from the request. To obtain a list of all available services for a given origin and destination, omit the Service and Packaging elements from the request. In this case the reply will contain every available service.
